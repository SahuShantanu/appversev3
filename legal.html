Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 24, 2026

At AppVerse, we recognize that physical privacy and data safety are fundamental. Our applications are engineered from the base level upwards to prevent telemetry data reporting and external surveillance tracking.

1. Information Collection and Telemetry

Our applications collect zero personal data, network location files, address book items, or device-specific identification tokens. We do not integrate data analysis tools or ad networking trackers in any APK file builds.

2. Local Storage and Keystore Protection

All database files, login hashes, configuration preferences, and cryptographic keys generated during app execution reside purely on the physical device storage. Sensitive identifiers are encrypted under the native hardware Android Keystore System and cannot be retrieved externally.

3. Policy Revisions

AppVerse reserves the right to modify this safety policy to accommodate native Android system upgrades. Any revisions will be published immediately on this portal.
